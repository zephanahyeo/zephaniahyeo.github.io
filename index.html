A few months after finishing high school, Zef and Alex found themselves living in a small sea shack on the east coast of Taiwan, with a stray kitten and a collection of field guides inherited from Zef's grandfather. Zef and Alex worked at a gas station in the evening, and in the mornings Zef fermented vegetables from his garden into rare relishes and liquors, while Alex hunted for herbs and mushrooms in the old growth forest. The following year, they began selling their culinary creations. Here in this small shop, we celebrate the forces of growth and decay that animate the outer wilds. Our ingredients come fresh from the mountain every morning, like they always have.
